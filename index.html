Courses We Offer

We offer three leading computer science and IT courses for students at ISMT College in Kathmandu.

BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineering (IT)

This three-year program, delivered through BTEC HND and the University of Sunderland, UK, covers software engineering, networking, data technology, AI, and Android mobile development, preparing students for careers in IT across industries.

BSc (Hons) Cyber Security and Digital Forensics

This three-year course, offered with the University of Sunderland, focuses on cybersecurity, network security, and digital forensics, equipping students with skills to protect digital information and investigate cybercrimes.

BTEC HND in Computing

This two-year program, leading to a BSc IT degree, offers practical training in software engineering, networking, and database management, with pathways in Network Engineering or Software Engineering, preparing students for IT careers or further study.

Facilities provide for Students by ISMT College

Canteen

ISMT College’s canteen offers a variety of nutritious meals and snacks in a welcoming setting, providing a relaxing space for students to socialize and recharge during their studies.

Auditorium

ISMT College’s auditorium is a state-of-the-art venue with advanced audio-visual systems, accommodating seminars, workshops, and events, fostering a dynamic learning and networking environment for students and professionals.

Library

ISMT College’s e-library and resource center provide access to a vast collection of digital and physical books, journals, and research materials, supporting students with modern tools for IT and computing studies in a quiet, technology-enhanced environment.
